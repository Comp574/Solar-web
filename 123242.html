How Solar Energy Works

Solar energy is harnessed using solar panels, which convert sunlight into electricity. Here's a step-by-step explanation of the process:

  1. Sunlight Absorption: Solar panels, made up of photovoltaic (PV) cells, absorb sunlight.
  2. Electricity Generation: The PV cells generate direct current (DC) electricity when exposed to sunlight.
  3. Conversion: An inverter converts the DC electricity into alternating current (AC) electricity, which powers your home or business.
  4. Usage and Storage: The electricity is used to power your appliances, and any excess can be stored in batteries or fed back into the grid.

By switching to solar energy, you not only save money on your energy bills but also contribute to a cleaner and greener planet.
